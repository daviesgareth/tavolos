what is tavolos

tavolos is the first platform offering data & insights that actually help your restaurant.

with our suite of tools built specifically for restaurants, you can be sure your business is in good hands. with free table reservations, premium data reporting, and an internal marketplace look no further than tavolos.

  • free reservations: why pay a fortune for a reservation platform with no guarantee? use our feature rich tool to manage your bookings, table plans, customer information, who's a VIP, and rate your diners.

  • reduce no-shows & increase loyalty: we all know that there's little more annoying than no shows. see which of your customers are regular no-show'ers, and which diners are worth going the extra mile for.

  • see how you compare: tavolos is the only platform that compares you directly to the market. see how your pricing, profit, spend per head compares to your competitors. see where your customers are coming from, how they're getting there and how they found you.

  • buy and sell locally: it's important to support local businesses. we're giving smaller suppliers a chance to take it to the big boys. browse local producers and suppliers to see how they can help your business remain local.
